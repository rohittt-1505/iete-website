PRINCIPAL'S INSIGHTS


Dr.Vilas Nitnaware
Principal, KCCEMSR

"The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is one of India's leading professional societies, advancing science and technology in electronics and telecommunication. The IETE Student Forum (ISF) fosters the holistic development of engineering students through events like conferences, workshops, guest lectures, and industrial visits, enhancing their technical knowledge and skills such as teamwork, lifelong learning, communication, and management. Our management and staff are dedicated to preparing students for global opportunities while fostering local engagement, ensuring successful placements in multinational companies. We organize seminars, workshops, and conferences to keep students updated with the latest scientific and technological developments. We believe in harmonizing personalities with their surroundings to pursue technical endeavors with an open mind. I am proud to congratulate the ISF team of KCCEMSR for being recognized as the Best ISF for the academic years 2020-21, 2021-22, and 2022-23. Under the guidance of Dr. Baban U. Rindhe and with the support of faculty coordinators, the team has excelled. Best wishes to the ISF team. Best wishes to the ISF team."

IETE - SF
Convener Message


Dr. Baban U. Rindhe
Immdt. Past Chairman, IETE Navi Mumbai, Head of EXTC Dept. & ISF Convener.

"I had the great opportunity to initiate the IETE Students Forum (ISF) for the Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering department at K.C. College of Engineering & Management Studies & Research in November 2018. The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE), established in 1953, is India’s premier professional society dedicated to the advancement of science, technology, electronics, telecommunications, and IT. The ISF aims to enhance engineering education standards and motivate students through extracurricular studies, workshops, projects, and seminars. The ultimate goal is for students to reflect their education in their professional responsibilities as graduates. Faculty roles adapt to be instructors, trainers, facilitators, or mentors to help students achieve these outcomes, focusing on skill development relevant to curriculam. I am proud to share that our ISF Center received the Best ISF Award for three consecutive academic years 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23. Our ISF Faculty Coordinator, Ms. Purnmia Vadak made continuous efforts to organize activities beyond classroom teaching. Under the leadership of students Mr. Avinash Rajak and Ms. Janhvi Arya, along with the ISF Team and members, they followed the guidance of the coordinators to achieve this success. I look forward to our ISF team achieving more milestones in the coming years. "

IETE - SF Faculty
CO-ordinator message


Asst.Prof. Purnima Vadak
EXTC Department

"It is with immense pleasure and pride that I had the privilege of guiding and collaborating with the ISF students as a Faculty Coordinator at K.C. College of Engineering & Management Studies & Research. Each student harbors remarkable potential, and through their involvement in ISF, they have identified and honed their strengths and addressed their weaknesses. Our dynamic ISF organized a variety of events, seamlessly blending technical and non-technical activities, along with innovative competitions to advance the knowledge and skills of participants. Additionally, we hosted numerous inter-college events. We are honored that KCCEMSR received the prestigious BEST ISF AWARD for three consecutive years, 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23. I extend my heartfelt gratitude to our ISF convenor and Head of EXTC department, Dr. Baban U. Rindhe, as well as our Principal, Dr. Vilas Nitnaware for their unwavering support. We eagerly anticipate an expanded and even more enthusiastic ISF in 2024-25. "
